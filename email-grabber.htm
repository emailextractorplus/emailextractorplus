Email Grabber is a program for automatic collection of email addresses.

Recently, electronic mailing of commercial offers on specific topics has been gaining popularity. To make your email messages as effective as possible, you must clearly determine which companies or websites may want to use your offer, as well as find out the email addresses of the persons who might be interested in your offer. This approach will make your electronic mailing targeted and save you a lot of time; if your expenses depend on the number of sent messages, it will also save you money.

Email Grabber lets you find out which companies or websites are related to a specific topic, collect the email addresses of each company’s contact persons who might be interested in your offer, and send your offer directly to those persons. The program can search by keywords to any depth specified by you (in any range of positions) via Google, Yahoo, Bing, and so on. Email Grabber can also import email addresses from Microsoft Outlook, text files, or other data sources.

Moreover, it can process email lists, namely filter by keyword and remove duplicate or wrong addresses. E-mail Grabber can export mailing lists to the Microsoft Excel format, so you can easily pass them to your email sending program.
